Summary

I have spent the last few years of my coding career training inmates and parolees to become successful developers for Banyan Labs and become gainfully employed.

This role has had me take on numerous other roles with our partner company Banyan Labs such as lead developer, lead architect, and various other dev team roles that needed to be filled at the time. In essence, I train the Banyan Labs team once they are released from prison.

I don't have a typical first part of my day, unless I'm assisting my students on the development team. Otherwise, I continue to further my coding knowledge through courses, reading documentation, and building/launching projects.

The web development team that I train typically codes in React/Gatsby or Vue for Front-End. For Back-End I teach a mixture of databases like SQL, Mongo, and Firebase.

The mobile application team that I train typically codes in React Native iOS or Android. Then using Express, Node, and usually Firebase we build our mobile apps.
